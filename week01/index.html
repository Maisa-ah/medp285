“During the day,
the light would make
this room seem a little sad,
despite the roses.”

Georges Perec lists out many objects that are unfamiliar to someone like me nowadays. The way the chapter is structured shows him creating a setting for his characters. However, given that he doesn’t create his characters by giving them personality, it feels as if anyone could fit with these objects. I believe this feels more nostalgic, as if he’s describing his own memories rather than creating one for these unknown characters.
