    Covid-19 database

  • Designing a database managed on IBM Cloud.
  • Problem statement: In recent events, there are people protesting against quarantine and the closing of non-essential businesses. The question we are trying to answer is: Did lockdown help flatten the curve to slow the spread of the coronavirus?
  • Data focuses on the USA, as that is where protests are the most prevalant. Attributes: state, hospital, patient, patient test result, patient status, patient treatment (hospitalized, in icu, on ventilator), bed capacity, icu capacity, ventilator capacity.
